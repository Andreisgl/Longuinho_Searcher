Under the terms of this Taylorist bargain, the worker surrendered his sense of craftsmanship and control over his own work in return for the right to express his " real" personality through consumption in the part of his life that still belonged to him. This bargain assumed the separation of work from the home. However arbitrary and abusive the boss may have been on the factory floor, when work was over the workers could at least escape his tyranny… he separation of work from home made a big difference to workers' liberty from their employers' wills.
