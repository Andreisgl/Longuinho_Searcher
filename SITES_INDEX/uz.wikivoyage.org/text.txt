Best described as an "outpost", the only meaningful sight in Stung Treng is watching the sun set and rise over the landscape.
The small 14th-century Boyana church (pictured)  in Sofia contains some very well preserved murals and is with its garden on the UNESCO World Heritage List.
In Cologne you can visit a chocolate museum.
