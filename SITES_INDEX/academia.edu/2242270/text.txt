Münster, Ugarit-Verlag, 2018, pages 243-257.Jaume Llop-RaduàDownload Free PDFView PDF2016 The weaponry of the Middle Assyrian army according to the written sources.Jaume Llop-RaduàThe present study discusses, the defensive and offensive weapons used by the Middle Assyrian warrior. The information proceeds mainly from the archival documentation. The defensive weapons were the helmet (qurpisu) or hat (kubšu), armour (*sariu) and the shield (arittu). The offensive weapons can be divided into those used for close combat and weapons for distance combat. The close combat weapons are the stick (haṭṭu), the whip (maḫittu), possibly the mace (kakku), the dagger or sword (patru), various types of axes (ḫaṣṣinnu, pāšu, *pāltu and ulmu) and the lance. Weapons for long range combat were the spear, the bow and arrows and the sling, used to throw projectiles.Download Free PDFView PDFSee Full PDFDownload PDFLoading PreviewSorry, preview is currently unavailable.
