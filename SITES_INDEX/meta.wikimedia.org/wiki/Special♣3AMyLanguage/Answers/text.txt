Thank you for your interest in the Wikimedia Foundation.
On the left are links that may answer your questions. 
For questions about specific Wikimedia projects and their processes, you may be interested in their community-maintained help  and contact pages. For questions about the MediaWiki software, MediaWiki's main page provides links to useful resources. 
For questions about the Wikimedia Foundation, see the links on the left, and this year's Annual Plan. If you cannot find an answer to your question among these resources, you may want to contact answerswikimediaorg (see process).
For general discussion about Wikimedia and its projects with the community, please join one of our public mailing lists, IRC channels, or community Discord servers.
