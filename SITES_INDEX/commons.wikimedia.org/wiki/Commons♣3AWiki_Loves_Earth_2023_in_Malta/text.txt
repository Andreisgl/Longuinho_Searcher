Wiki Loves Earth in Malta 2023
Wiki Loves Earth is an international photographic competition to promote natural heritage sites and creatures around the world, it is the second largest photography competition in the world. Follow these simple steps to take part:
PrizesThe top 10 winning photos taken at one of the 300 protected natural sites in Malta will be published in the Times of Malta. The top 10 photos will also receive a goody bag from Wikimedia Community Malta during the 2023 Malta Book Festival and go through to the international competition. Winners will be announced by in August/September 2023.
How to enterYou can share as many of your photos as you like and these can have been taken at any time, you don't have to live in Malta to take part.Please take photos with GPS turned on, if possible, both for photos of sites or creatures.Images should not contain watermarks and they will be under CC-by-SA 4.0 license.
Get startedWhen you create a Wikimedia account please include your email address so we can contact you if you win.You can also enter images for any of the other participating countries.The competition runs from 1 May - 31 July 2023.Click here to upload your images.Upload your photos here
