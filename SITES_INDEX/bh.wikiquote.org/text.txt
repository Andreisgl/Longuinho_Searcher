Wq > bih
SIL/ISO 639-3: bihEthnologue: bihGlottolog: bih
 The code "bih" does not refer to a specific language, but to a collection of languages, namely the Bihari languages.
Languages in this collective include:
Angika (anp)
Bajjika (no code)
Bhojpuri (bho)
Khortha (no code)
Kudmali (kyw)
Magahi (mag)
Maithili (mai)
Panchpargania (tdb)
Sadri (sck)
Tharu languages
Dangaura Tharu (thl)
Kathoriya Tharu (tkt)
Rana Tharu (thr)
Chitwania Tharu (the)
Kochila Tharu (thq)
Buksa Tharu (tkb)
Sonha (soi)
Kumhali (kra)
