The Wikimedia muivement, or simply Wikimedia, is the global commonty o contributors tae Wikimedia Foondation projects. The muivement wis creatit aroond Wikipaedia's commonty, an haes syne expandit tae the ither Wikimedia projects, includin the commons projects Wikimedia Commons an Wikidata, an volunteer software developers contributin tae MediaWiki. These volunteers are supportit bi numerous organizations aroond the warld, includin the Wikimedia Foondation, related chapters, thematic organizations, an uiser groups.
The name "Wikimedia", a compoond o wiki an media, wis coined bi American author Sheldon Rampton in a post tae the Inglis mailin leet in Mairch 2003,[1] three month efter Wiktionary became the seicont wiki-based project hostit on Jimmy Wales' platform, an three month afore the Wikimedia Foondation wis annooncit an incorporatit.[2][3] "Wikimedia" mey an aw refer tae the Wikimedia projects.
