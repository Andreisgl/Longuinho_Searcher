Le rôle des personnels de direction2019 • Raniha OultacheThe digital transition of the local public educational establishment. The role of the school head. Digital technology rocks the school institution, its methods of learning and its organizational structure. As a manager, the school head’s role is to facilitate and carry out the digital strategy in order to implement it within the school which he/she is responsible of. This research focuses on the digital transition of local public education establishment and the related ways of governance and steering. It discusses the conditions of practice and the school head’s missions as well as the managerial practices to be adopted regarding a young digital or hyper connected generation, the digitalization of all their processes of management and administration, the Web 2.0 culture in human relationships, the ubiquity of information that makes one wonders about the Education missions, the limits of the academic conventions facing the digital practices and new responsibilities (such as ecological, exclusion or security subjects…). Initiating a digital transition project means to highlight the establishment and to strengthen its links with the territorial communities. The school head must develop digital skills and be able to measure the challenges of data processing and their use in learning methods. His/her skills as a manager must also be based on collaborative, nimble and project management methods. The head must also encourage innovative practices, as vehicles for transformation and change.
