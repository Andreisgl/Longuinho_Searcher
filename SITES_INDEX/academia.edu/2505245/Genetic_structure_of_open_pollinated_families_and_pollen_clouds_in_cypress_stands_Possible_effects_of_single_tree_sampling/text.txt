Papageorgioufiles.figshare.comThe progenies and pollen clouds from two cypress stands with different genetic structures were analysed. The genetic structures among generations were compared. There were not large differences found in the allelic structures. However, the heterozygosity proportions have followed a certain differentiation trend, which is explained as an effect of single tree sampling.
