Refining & Reimagining Your Brand's Storytelling 
Take readers on an inspiring journey while showcasing your products or services to Forbes’ influential global audiences. Ideal for luxury and travel brands, Connoisseur is an artful form of custom storytelling whose impactful design catches the attention of Forbes readers.

Connoisseur Capabilities
Tell the story behind a product or explain to the world how your product reflects your brand’s identity. Connoisseur is where brand storytelling and product marketing combine to create design-forward, custom content that often places your stunning product photography at the center of the experience. Prominent calls-to-action that direct consumers to ecommerce websites are an option for brands that want to funnel consumers closer to making a purchase.
