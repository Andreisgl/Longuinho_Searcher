[MediaWiki-announce] Announcing MediaWiki 1.32.0
Mukunda Modell
                11 Jan
                
                    2019
                
            
                11 Jan
                
                '19
                
            
10:11 a.m.
 
I'm pleased to announce the release of MediaWiki version 1.32.0. Release
notes are updated and archives have been uploaded to releases.wikimedia.org.
Thanks to everyone who helped out with this release, especially thanks to
those who tested out the release candidates and provided feedback, as well
as the developers who worked hard to get several important fixes merged in
time for the 1.32 final.  To see what's changed in 1.32, see the release
notes[1].
Below you will find download links which lead directly the source for this
release, either with or without bundled extensions.
**********************************************************************
Download:
https://releases.wikimedia.org/mediawiki/1.32/mediawiki-1.32.0.tar.gz
Download without bundled extensions:
https://releases.wikimedia.org/mediawiki/1.32/mediawiki-core-1.32.0.tar.gz
Patch to previous version (1.32.0-rc.2):
https://releases.wikimedia.org/mediawiki/1.32/mediawiki-1.32.0.patch.gz
GPG signatures:
https://releases.wikimedia.org/mediawiki/1.32/mediawiki-core-1.32.0.tar.gz.…
https://releases.wikimedia.org/mediawiki/1.32/mediawiki-1.32.0.tar.gz.sig
https://releases.wikimedia.org/mediawiki/1.32/mediawiki-1.32.0.patch.gz.sig
Public keys:
https://www.mediawiki.org/keys/keys.html
Release Notes
https://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Release_notes/1.32
