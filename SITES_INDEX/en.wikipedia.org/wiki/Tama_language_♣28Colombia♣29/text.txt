Extinct indigenous Tucanoan language of Colombia
Not to be confused with Tama language (Chad).
TamaNative toColombiaRegionOrteguaza RiverExtinct(date missing)Language familyTucanoan
Western(?)TamaLanguage codesISO 639-3tenGlottologtama1340
Tama is an extinct indigenous Tucanoan language of Colombia.[1] It was spoken in the regions of Vicente, Orteguaza River and Caquetá Region.
