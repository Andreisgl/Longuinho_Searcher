Summary 
Wikivoyage Mailing List
The mailing list for Wikivoyage, the Wikimedia project dedicating to making freely licensed information about travel destination and other travel-related topics available to everyone on the planet, in their own language.
This list may discuss ongoing site management or technical issues.
To contact the list owners, use the following email address: wikivoyage-l-owner@lists.wikimedia.org
