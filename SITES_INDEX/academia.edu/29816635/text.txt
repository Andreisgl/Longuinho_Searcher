Remains of the dwellings, household pits and also complex on treatment of flint are excavated. The basic category of finds from a settlement are the sculptured ceramics, the finds of pottery vessels are rare. Metallic objects are not chaste, that partly could be compensated by the wide distribution of the flint wares.
