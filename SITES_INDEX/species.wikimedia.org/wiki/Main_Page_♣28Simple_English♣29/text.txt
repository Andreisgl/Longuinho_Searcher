Welcome to Wikispecies, a free directory of life!
Wikispecies is a free site of species. It covers Animalia, Plantae, Fungi, Bacteria, Archaea, Protista and all other kinds of life. So far we have 848,161 articles.
Wikispecies is free, because life is public domain!
If you have any questions about Wikispecies, please sign up at the Wikispecies mailing list:
mailinglist
Look at the Wikispecies:Help for help on creating pages.
See the Wikipedia article on taxonomy for help on the Linnaean classification of species.
See the Village pump to talk about the project.
See the done and to do page to see goals.
See Wikispecies FAQ for answers to questions that are asked a lot.
See the image rules on where to upload pictures and images.
