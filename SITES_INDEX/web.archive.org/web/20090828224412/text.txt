glam rock
glam rock
noun 
Definition:
 
U.K. 1970s pop music: a style of pop music in the U.K. in the 1970s performed by singers and musicians wearing outrageous clothes, makeup, hairstyles, and platform-soled boots. Its most famous exponents were the singers Gary Glitter and Marc Bolan and the band Sweet.
