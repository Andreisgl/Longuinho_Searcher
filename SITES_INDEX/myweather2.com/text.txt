Country Overview For Trinidad and TobagoThe climate in Trinidad and Tobago is  typically tropical; rainy season (June to December). The terrain: mostly plains with some hills and low mountains. For Port Of Spain  in January the daily average maximum temperatures is 31°C with the average minimum 21°C, while in June the average maximum is 32°C with a minimum of 24°C. The wettest month for Port Of Spain  is July with an average of 308.1mm of precipitation falling while the driest month is March with 30.1mm falling.
