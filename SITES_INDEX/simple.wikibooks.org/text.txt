Welcome to Simple English Wikibooks!
We are working to write textbooks in Simple English. This project was started on 25 July 2004, and now there are 330 textbook pages in Simple English – you can start making more yourself! Visit the community portal to learn about the Wikibooks community, or look at the bookshelves. If you need help with Wikibooks, go to the help section. This project is still very small, so if you need any help, contact an administrator or ask for help at Simple English Wikipedia or at Simple talk. If you want a book you don't see, add it to this list of requested books.
Wikibooks for children are at Wikijunior.
All the text here is free (open content) for you or anyone else to use and licensed with Creative Commons Attribution/Share Alike license. This is explained in Terms of Use page.
