Wikimēdia weorc
Wē sind gaderung tīdgiefera hwæs ǣrende is tō būenne webstede mid gengum nīwlicum, limpendum, spellweorðum, and gefeormiendum gewierðe wiþūtan wēnan.
Eall gewierðe gewriten for Wikispelle is in þǣm gemǣnlicum rīce.  In þǣm þe wē dōþ ūre gewierðe findendlic for frēore edwīdmǣrsunge and frēom bryce, wē wēnaþ tō geforðienne tō woruldlicum fingerlicum gewierðehēddærne.
Wikispell rōmaþ ēac tō wrītenne spell of hlotlēasum wēnan.
Wikispell þearf þīnes! Wē willaþ scieppan missenlicne gemǣnscipe in þǣm þe burgsǣtan fullwīde of þǣre worulde cunnon midwyrcan tō gecȳðenne þæt spell on wīdre fāgunge gangendra belimpa. 
Gif þu wilt wrītan, gewīdmǣrsian, oþþe ādihtan gewritu, nēos þā weorcstōwe and rǣd þā Inlādunge tō Wikispelle.
Nōt: Hwonne þu giefst gewierðe tō Wikispelle, þu āgiefst eall wīdmǣrsungriht þǣrofer.
