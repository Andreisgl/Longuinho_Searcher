Enamels are composite objects in which glass and metal are intimately... moreThe aim of this group is to facilitate the exchange of information on the study and conservation of enamels on metal among glass and metal conservation experts. Enamels are composite objects in which glass and metal are intimately connected and consequently lead to complex conservation issues. Experts who study and conserve ena-melled metal objects are generally specialists in only one of the two materials and need complementary information to conserve and treat these precious and delicate objects. The group, made of 115 professional members from around the world, meets this year in Barcelona. It is a tribute to the enamelling tradition of the city, which has a notable presence of this type of objects in collections.
