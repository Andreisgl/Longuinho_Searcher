English[edit]
English Wikipedia has an article on:bilingualismWikipedia 
Etymology[edit]
bilingual +‎ -ism
Noun[edit]
bilingualism (countable and uncountable, plural bilingualisms)
The condition of being bilingual; the ability to speak two languages.
