DescriptionSet Knowledge Free mural illustration.svg
English:  "Set Knowledge Free" mural illustrated by Tomi Um visually summarizes the global cultural impact of Wikipedia. The artist shows different knowledge topics contained in browser windows: history, social movements, nature, sports, technology, the environment, and space. The ~70 ft wide mural appears on the entry hallway of the Wikimedia Foundation offices in San Francisco.
