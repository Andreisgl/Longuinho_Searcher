London
Yunaeted Kingdom i kaontri long Yurop. Kapital blong hem i London. Hemi wan memba blong Kommonwelt. King blong Yunaeted Kingdom hemi Charles III, mo praem minista blong Yunaeted Kingdom hemi Rishi Sunak. Populaesen blong hem i stap araon 66 040 229 (2017)
