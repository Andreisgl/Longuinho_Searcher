Norwegian Wikisource reaches 50% of pr-texts 
posted by ThomasV 09:26, 20 April 2010 (UTC)Reply[reply]
In an effort to improve the quality and trustability of Wikisource, texts are now 
being backed with scans at many subdomains. This process is particularly advanced 
at the Norwegian Wikisource, where a milestone has been reached yesterday : more 
than 50% of the texts proposed at no.ws are now transcluded from the "Page" namespace 
and backed with scans. Congratulations!
