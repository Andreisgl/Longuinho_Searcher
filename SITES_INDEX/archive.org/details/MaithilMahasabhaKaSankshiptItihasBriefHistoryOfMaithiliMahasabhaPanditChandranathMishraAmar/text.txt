Maithil Mahasabha ka
Sankahipt Itihas (A Brief History of Maithili Mahasabha) was authored by noted Maithili
poet author and critic Pandit Chandranath Mishra ‘Amar’. Maithil Mahasabha ka
Sankahipt Itihas is an important research work in Maithili which informs about
this important organization in Mithila established in 1910. The organization
played an important role in social reform, spreading education and building consensus
for the cause of nation.
