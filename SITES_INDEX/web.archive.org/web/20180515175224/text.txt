About:
Negaraku (My Country) was picked as the Malaysian anthem at the time of the Federation of Malaya's independence from Britain in 1957. The selection of the anthem followed an international competition, and was adopted from the state anthem of Perak, one of the constituent states of Malaysia. The melody of the anthem was an adaptation of a piece by the nineteenth-century French composer Pierre-Jean de Béranger.

My country, the land where my blood has spilt.
