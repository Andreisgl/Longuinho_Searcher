Aloha mai kākou mai ke alaula a ka mōlehulehu o ka lā, Welina mai!
ʻO kēia he puke wehewehe ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi ma ia wahi. Ma aneʻi, hiki ke wehewehe i nā huaʻolelo Hawaiʻi ma ia ʻōlelo a i ʻole hiki ke unuhi i nā ʻōlelo ʻē aʻe ma kēia honua nei. Ma o kēia papahana, e ulu ana ka ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi a me ka ʻiʻini i loko o ka naʻau o nā kanaka maoli oiwi o ka ʻāina makuahine lua ʻole. E ʻoluʻolu, e kōkua a kaʻana i kou mau manaʻo Hawaiʻi no ka mōhala ʻana o ka ʻōlelo makuahine. E lawe mai i nā hoa ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi a noinoi iā lākou no ko lākou kōkua ʻana me kēia papahana koʻikoʻi loa. Mahalo no kou kipa ʻana mai i kēia wahi. E mālama pono!
"I ka ʻōlelo no ke ola, I ka ʻōlelo no ka make" -Mary Kawena Pukuʻi
