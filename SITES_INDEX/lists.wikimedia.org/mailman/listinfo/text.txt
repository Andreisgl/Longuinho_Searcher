WikimediaAnnounce-l wikimediaannounce-l@lists.wikimedia.org
Summary 
Announcements and reports of the Wikimedia movement
A moderated list for any announcements of relevance to a large audience of Wikimedia community members, friends and interested observers. As of July 2021, the list had more than 1,350 subscribers. Announcements can be made by any member of the Wikimedia community. See the list page for further information and policies.
To contact the list owners, use the following email address: wikimediaannounce-l-owner@lists.wikimedia.org
