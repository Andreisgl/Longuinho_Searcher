The following is a list of the various established and proposed wikibooks for various languages. For wiktionaries, see Wiktionary:Wiktionary:Multilingual coordination.
See ISO 639 Language Codes to see what languages the abbreviations stand for, and where the codes below originated.  (Note not all codes are ISO 639.)
Note: just adding a link below does not suddenly spring a wiki into existence. :-) Currently, Wikibooks' volunteer developers are overworked and understaffed. Your patience is appreciated, and pitching in with development is even more welcome.
If a wiki becomes active, please do move a link to all the relevant Wikipedia pages, and help promote the effort by announcing the effort on the Textbook-L mailing list and perhaps also Wikimedia News.
The languages listed below are Wikibooks which have been created. Their progress should be tracked at Wikibooks/Table. To start a new Wikibooks in a language not listed here, please see How to start a new Wikibooks portal.
