Svensk titel: Folkbibliotekarier och nya medier: en diskursanalys av folkbibliotekariers inställning till nya medier Engelsk titel: Librarians and new media: a discourse analysis of librarians' attitudes towards new media Författare: Maria Björklund och Lisa Roos Kollegium: 3 Färdigställt: 2004 Handledare: Kerstin Rydsjö Abstract: The aim of this Master's thesis is to examine librarians' opinions about media. Four questions are asked: What kind of different attitudes to new media has librarians had? How is new media discussed in relationship to each other in the debate? What mechanisms and underlying causes control the discourse for new media in public libraries?
