The video WikiGuides are instructional videos, each about 7 minutes long, in Italian, produced by Wikimedia Italia to help people understand Wikimedia projects. We provide them under CC-BY-SA with raw video, original and English script and subtitles and help to produce localized versions as far as possible. Video are listed also in the Wikimedia Bookshelf.
