Yogır lar pır Thürık lar. This series of pages is for a wikipedia in the en:Western Yugur language.
Your Western Yugur input here will be appreciated. The request for the creation of the Western Yugur Wikipedia will be approved when there is sufficient article content. When approved, all content here would be moved to the actual Wikipedia.
Because this is a wikipedia all articles here must begin with the format [[Wp/ybe/Name of article]].  For example, an article about "Western Yugur" should be titled here as [[Wp/ybe/Western Yugur]].  (Of course in the actual wikipedia, the article would be titled as Western Yugur
