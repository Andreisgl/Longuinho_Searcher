Quote of the Week of October 5–October 11 
"Some people think you have more privileges than other teenagers because of what you do, and that's not it at all. It's actually the opposite." - Miley Cyrus
Simple: Some people think you have a better life than anyone else because you're famous. But that is not true. It is actually the opposite.
 What Is Wikiquote?
Wikiquote is a collection of quotes, run by the Wikimedia Foundation. Wikiquote in Simple English are these same quotes, but with easier to understand meanings of them.
You can help us by changing the pages here, or starting new ones. If you don't know how to make one, you may ask for it. If you have any questions, feel free to ask them at Simple talk. Welcome!
