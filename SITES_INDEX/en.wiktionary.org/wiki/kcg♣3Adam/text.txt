Tyap[jhyuk]
A̱nietkhwop-a̱niet a̱ghyang swan a̱ dam tazwa kyang.
Yei[jhyuk]
Za̱FBS: /dɑ́m/
A̱nwuat: Si̱sak nang á̱ ngyei "dam" a̱ni.
Ta̱m[jhyuk]
dam, ku dam, ka̱n dam, bye dam, ba̱ dam, wu dam, na dam, ndam
Dam yet byia̱ a̱fi̱fa tazwa kyang hu nang a̱yin jhyung a̱ni ku a̱bung nang a̱yin swuo a̱ni.
A̱yang nung dam si̱sak gu mbyin nung a̱ni.
Meri, yok dam a̱ la̱u bah, kalak a na khwu.
Nyi dam da̱ a̱lyiat nang n dyiat ma̱ng a̱nyin tazwa a̱ni.
