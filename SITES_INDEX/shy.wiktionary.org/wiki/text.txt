Ansuf yis-k ɣer Wikasegzawal.
I fus ntamɛawna, i tečučed i tacawit.
Netcnin d Wikasegzawal n tacawit atafem iwalen n icawiyen, atafem matta taxassem amawal, ay-a atafem dis iwalen d medut ay ict ad nedjem adis yerni matta yixs, mata axssam arnit awal itassnam.
ameqnin
Wikasegzawal yers ibedd ɣef yiǧ n ṛṛay :  tira n isebtar ur lazem ca ad yili dis ttaḥeyyuz (arawsan) s yiǧ n ṛṛay xaṭi wayeḍ. Ger lbal nnek nnem: ay-a ur yenni ca ad nari s ṛṛay mammek txemmimen labas n midden. Ay-a yexs ad yini ad ilin dis ṛṛay n kul yiǧ, ḥata ur yeḥli ca qaɛ neɣ qli qič. Tira s wul d taẓuri yexsen tirmit d ttaɛewwed.
Wikasegzawal ur yetḥewwas ca utlay yeḥlan, yexs ad yuc amkan i wawal n kul yiǧ yellan d mammek yettutlay.
