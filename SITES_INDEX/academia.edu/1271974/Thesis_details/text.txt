Mémoire de deuxième année de Master d'Histoire de la philosophie, Université Paris-I (2015-2016)2016 • Lucas PasquetDissertation of Master degree in Philosophy realised in 2015-2016. I tried to analyse what I have felt to be a strong but underestimated tendancy in Nietzsche's work: his antisubstantialism. I have found some indirect buddhist influence in its development and tried to lead a comparison between both approaches in order to evaluate the strengh of this perspective which permits to read Nietzsche in an other way. A way which destroys the fundational approaches of the self, and a way which opens a possibility to newly consider the self and personal identity.
