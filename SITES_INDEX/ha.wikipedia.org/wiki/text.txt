Alhaji Abubakar ImamAbubakar Imam (Taimako·bayani) O.B.E, C.O.N, L.L.D, (Hon.) N.N.M.C,  shahararren marubucin Hausa ne wanda ya wallafa littattafan Hausa da dama, cikinsu akwai irinsu Magana Jari Ce, Ruwan Bagaja da sauransu. Marubucin ya samu shahara a Duniyar Nazarin Harshen Hausa. Ya zamo edita na farko a gidan jaridar Arewa, Gaskiya Ta Fi Kwabo yanzu haka a na nazarin wallafe-wallafen da ya yi a manya da kananan makarantu a Nijeriya. Abubakar Imam shine marubucin littattafan Hausa na farko-farko a karni na( 20), a tsakanin watan Afrilu zuwa watan Augusta na shekara ta alif(1934).
