Maya BoromMonash University,  Faculty of Arts, School of Media, Film and Journalism,  Graduate StudentInternational Law+3A lawyer and academic with research and teaching areas of interest in criminology and law, including terrorism, AI and IHL, constitutional and administrative law, human rights and media/cinema. Maya's doctoral research focuses on incursions of international law and human rights through cinematic and media depictions of terrorism, exploring concepts around digital criminology, law and screen studies.Maya is a Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts, a Fellow of the Global Research Network for the Crime and Terrorism Think Tank Program for exceptional early career scholars ​around the world working ​in law and in the humanities as well as a research member of AVERT (Addressing Violent Extremism and Radicalisation to Terrorism) Research Network.
