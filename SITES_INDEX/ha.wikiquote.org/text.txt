Hausa/هَوُسَا - Barka da zuwa!
Hausa WikiquoteBarka da zuwa Shafin Hausa Wikiquote inda zaku iya hakayo magana ta fasaha da wani yayi ko karin magana acikin harshen Hausa. Acikin wannan shafin akwai maganganu da kalamai na fasaha da wasu daga cikin jigogin al'umma sukayi. Zaku iya taimakawa wajen sanya irin wadannan kalamai masu ma'ana a wannan shafin.
Alhamis 31 Agusta 2023
Article #160,447 :
Wannan shine babban shafin Hausa Wikiquote dake a Wimedia Incurbator.
