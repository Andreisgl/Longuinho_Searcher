English[edit]
English Wikipedia has an article on:multilingualismWikipedia 
Etymology[edit]
From multilingual +‎ -ism.
Noun[edit]
multilingualism (usually uncountable, plural multilingualisms)
the condition of being multilingual; the ability to speak multiple languages
Synonyms[edit]
multilinguality
Antonyms[edit]
monolingualism, unilingualism
Related terms[edit]
bilingualismmonolingualismtrilingualism
