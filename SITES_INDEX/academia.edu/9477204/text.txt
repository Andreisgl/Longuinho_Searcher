I also used to use various films in classes or as part of club activities in the business school. I have collected over the years what I think is a good list of how business is depicted in film over the years (starting in 1915). My list is not meant to be comprehensive but an overview of what I think are quite good examples of the view of business by filmmakers with the proviso that the movies have to be reasonably good.
