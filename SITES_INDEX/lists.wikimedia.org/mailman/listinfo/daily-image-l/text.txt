Summary 
Wikimedia Commons Picture of the Day
Wikimedia CommonsPicture of the Day
In your inbox, every day!This is an announcement-only list where subscribers will receive one email per day, containing a link to (and details about) the Wikimedia Commons' Picture of the Day. These "Featured Pictures" are high-quality, freely-licensed media, usually created by Wikimedians or sourced from the public domain. They are recognised by the Wikimedia Commons community as the best of the best.
Questions & answers can be found at https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Commons:Daily-image-l.
To contact the list owners, use the following email address: daily-image-l-owner@lists.wikimedia.org
