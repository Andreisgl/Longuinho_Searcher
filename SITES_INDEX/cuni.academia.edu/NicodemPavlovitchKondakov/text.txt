Various works of art of ancient Russia: crosses, plaques, pendants, earrings, rings, temporal rings and other items. The book contains a description of the various treasures: Ryazan (1822), Kiev (1824), Pereyaslavl (1855), Chernigov (1883 and 1887.) And others.
