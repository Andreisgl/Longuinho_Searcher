Call for Feedback on the Open Source Strategy & Policy
 Comments
The National Information Technology Authority (NITA-U) in conjunction with the ICT Association of Uganda (ICTAU) are organizing a workshop on IT Innovation and Open Source in Uganda on 1st October 2014 starting at 8:30 am to 4:30 pm at Statistics House Conference hall on 1st floor.
Prior to this, they would like to collect your feedback on the Open Source Strategy & Policy. Below are the draft documents on the Open Source Strategy & Policy for your review, we welcome your feedback.
Open Source Strategy V0.3 2014-09-21
Open Source Policy V0.3 2014-09-21 
You can send your feedback to mariam@ictau.ug or jeddy@ictau.ug or gloria.Kansiime@nita.go.ug
