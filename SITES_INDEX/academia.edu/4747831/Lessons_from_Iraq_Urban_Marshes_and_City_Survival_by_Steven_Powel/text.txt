Lessons from Iraq: Urban Marshes and City Survival by Steven PowelRequest PDFLessons from Iraq: Urban Marshes and City Survival by Steven PowelScience Daily, 2012Jennifer R PournelleAbstractA scientist at the University of South Carolina is continuing to build the case that natural wetlands, rather than irrigated fields, are the fertile ground from which cities initially emerged in Mesopotamia. And her conclusions about the importance of wetlands to a sustainable urban environment -- or, in fact, any environment -- have particular resonance in southern Iraq.
