Dagens billede
1. september 2023
Panoramic view (span of approx. 150°) of the city of Cusco, Peru. The city, located near the Sacred Valley of the Andes mountain range, is the capital of the Cusco Province, has a population of 428,450 inhabitants and its elevation is around 3,400 metres (11,200 ft). The site was the historic capital of the Inca Empire from the 13th until the 16th-century Spanish conquest and was declared in 1983 a UNESCO World Heritage Site.+/− [da], +/− [en]
Dette er en oversigt over de billeder der vises som Dagens billede på forsiden for Commons. Denne side giver mulighed for oversættelse af billedteksten til så mange sprog som muligt.
