Author namespace created at fr.ws [edit]
posted by ThomasV 11:50, 22 August 2009 (UTC)Reply[reply]
Following other subdomains, the french Wikisource requested and obtained the creation of an "Auteur" Namespace.
More than 2000 author pages were moved from "ns-main" to this namespace. A robot is being used to update all the links.
