Objectives[edit]
This group aims to encourage the uptake of wiki activity amongst Maltese communities, through the implementation of various wiki activities, projects, and training & outreach events.
Activities[edit]
Wikimedia Community Malta carries out a number of wiki-related activities, including:

See a list of reports related to the activities.
History[edit]
Wikimedia Community Malta was first established in October 2015. It is a voluntary organisation registered with the Malta Council for the Voluntary Sector.

How to participate[edit]
We are very keen to involve as many people as possible in the Wikimedia Community Malta User Group. If you are interested participating please just contact us through the talk page for this page or the Wikimedia Community Malta wiki.
Wikimedia Community Malta meets on a regular basis to discuss the projects listed on this page - everybody is welcome to join us and contribute to the growth of this group.
