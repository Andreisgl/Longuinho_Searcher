Di tins wey im dey tich na abaut skul wey pipul wey no bi smol pikin dey go, how difren pipul dey du dia difren difren kolsho for di wold an abaut som patikula ples-dem. Atikul for todey
Gun langwej (Gun: gungbe) na langwej wey Ogu pipul wey dey for Benin Replublic an Naija dey tok. Gun langwej dey among di langwej grup wey dem dey kol Gbe. Di langwej near oda langwej like Fon, Agbome, Kpase, Maxi and Weme (Ouémé). Na dis langwej dem dey tok pas for Benin an dem dey yuz am tich for some skul-dem for Ouémé Department for Benin...Read-am dey go...
