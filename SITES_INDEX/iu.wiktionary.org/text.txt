This is a Wiktionary in Inuktitut (ᐃᓄᒃᑎᑐᑦ/Inuktitut). If you know the language and can help to create a dictionary in Inuktitut, you can do it. To start organizing this, just create some entries or to learn more about this project you can have a look at Wiktionary in English (ᖃᓪᓗᓈᑎᑐᑦ/qallunaatitut) or at Wikipedia in Inuktitut. You can click as well on any of the links (highlighted in blue) to the many Wiktionaries in different languages that can be found below on the left corner of this page
