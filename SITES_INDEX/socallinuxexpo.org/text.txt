SCALE 21x
Submitted by Ilan Rabinovitch on Tue, 2023-07-18 14:49 
SCaLE 21x – the 21st Annual Southern California Linux Expo – will take place on March 14-17, 2024 at the Pasadena Convention Center in Pasadena, CA.
SCaLE is the largest community-run open-source and free software conference in North America. It is held annually in the greater Los Angeles area.
Volunteer!  Interested in helping to plan and/or to volunteer for SCALE 20x? Get in touch by either filling out this form or sending us an email.
Attend!  Register to attend the conference. Expo is open Friday - Sunday. Talks and workshops are Thursday - Sunday.
Present! The SCALE 21x call for proposals is open Aug 1, 2023 - November 1, 2023.
Sponsor! Sponsorship and exhibitor opportunities are available for commercial and non-profit exhibitors. Email us for details.
Stay!  Room blocks at local hotels will be available in the coming weeks.
