Italian Wikisource reaches 30,000 validated pages [edit]
posted by --εΔω 22:29, 10 January 2013 (UTC)
With page 203 from the novel A challenge in the North Pole by Emilio Salgari, the Italian Wikisource has validated 30,000 pages checked with scans in Page mode by two different contributors.
