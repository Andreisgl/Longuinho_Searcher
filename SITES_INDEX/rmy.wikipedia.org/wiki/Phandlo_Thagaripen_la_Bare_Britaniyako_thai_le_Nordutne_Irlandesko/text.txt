1Ando PhTh, varesave chiba prinjardyoven sar thanutne chiba. O anav le PhTh-sko andi fishtesavi chib:
Welshikani chib: Teyrnas Unedig Prydain Fawr a Gogledd Iwerddon
Shkotikani Gelik: An Rìoghachd Aonaichte na Breatainn Mhòr agus Eirinn a Tuath

Shkots chib: Unitit Kinrick o Great Breetain an Northren Ireland
Kornishikani chib: An Rywvaneth Unys a Vreten Veur hag Iwerdhon Glédh
O Phandlo Thagaripen la Bari Britaniyako tay le Nordutne Irlandesko (anglezikani chib: United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland) si yek forovipnasko phandipen andar shtar thema: England, Shkotlando tay Wales ando dvip Bari Britaniya vi Nordutno Irland ando dvip Irland. P.T. xarnes janglyovel sar o Phandlo Thagaripen (anglezikanes: United Kingdom vay U.K.). O them si xulayardo sar yek konstitucikano thagaripen tay sar yek yektutno them. O Phandlo Thagaripen si les vi teritorurya intyal le deryavurende sar o Jibraltaro, le Dvipa Falkland.
