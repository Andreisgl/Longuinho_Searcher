Editorial Notes
[The colony of Trinidad and the colony of Tobago were amalgamated in 1888 to form the colony of Trinidad and Tobago, which became independent on Aug. 31, 1962, under the same name. Use "Trinidad and Tobago" for publs. after 1888.  Headings for the government of Tobago since 1980 are found under: Tobago (Trinidad and Tobago).]
[Old catalog headings: Trinidad [used for publs. through Aug. 31, 1962]; Tobago [used for publs. through Aug. 31, 1962]; Trinidad and Tobago [used Sept. 1, 1962- ]]
