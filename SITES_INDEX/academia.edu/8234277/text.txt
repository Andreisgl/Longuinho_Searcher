The trial study was carried out in a farrow to finishing farm (500 sows) at Amphoe maung, Lamphun province. The experiment composed of 180 weaning pigs (30 days of age). The samples were divided into 4 groups (each group per 15 pigs; 3 replications) which these had a similar to average live weight (P>0.05).
