The Malaysia Lawn Bowls Federation has applied a mixture of conventional wisdom, and adopted innovative approaches in the management of its affairs. Our vision is "To Be the Best in the World". Our mission is "To enhance individual and team achievement through providing world class coaching and training programmes. BACKGROUNDThe Malaysia Lawn Bowls Federation was registered on 13th March 1997 by the Register of Societies (Mohiyaddin bin Che Jan), an officer in the Malaysian Administrative and Diplomatic Service. The Malaysia Lawn Bowls Federation is governed by a constitution, which underwent an important amendment in 1998. The federation had been re-registered on 8th may 1998 (Registration No. 0017/98) under the Sports Development Act 1997.AddressKompleks Lawn Bowls Lot 3959 Jalan 1/70 D Bukit KiaraKuala Lumpur,Wilayah Persekutuan Malaysia 60000 Telephone603-2093 5355Fax603-2093 2455Emailrambleoxley@yahoo.com Websitehttp://www.bowlsmalaysia.com ContactMr Ramble Haji Dallan, Secretary
