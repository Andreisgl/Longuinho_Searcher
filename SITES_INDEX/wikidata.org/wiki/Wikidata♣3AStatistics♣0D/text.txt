Statistical hub
Pointers to all sorts of statistics. About Wikidata content, the Wikidata community, type of content, etc.
How big is Wikidata?
Wikidata currently contains 106,543,189 items. 1,965,854,450 edits have been made since the project launch.
You can find additional details at wikidata-todo/stats.php and stats.wikimedia.org/#/wikidata.org.
Who edits Wikidata?
There are currently 23,521 active users. You can find more data at Wikimedia Statistics.
Wikipedia statistics
The Wikidata Concepts Monitor (WDCM) provides a full overview of Wikidata usage for more than 800 Wikimedia projects. Wikidata helps uncover a lot of facts about Wikipedia content. Find more at Wikidata:Statistics/Wikipedia.
