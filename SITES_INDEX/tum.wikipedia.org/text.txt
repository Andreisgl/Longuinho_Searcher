Nkhani ya mwahuno
Vimbuza ni gule uwo ukuvinika pakati pa ŵaTumbuka ŵaku Malaŵi na Zambia, na pachoko ku mpoto kwa charu cha Tanzania. Gule uwu una ntchito zinandi ndipo imoza mwa ntchito ya nkhuchizga nthenda za mizimu zakupambanapambana, ndiposo ni gule wachisangulusko. Vimbuza vikuvinika chomene mu boma la Rumphi, Mzimba, Lundazi, Lumezi, Chasefu na Karonga. Vimbuza yichali kuchitika mu vigaŵa vya ku mizi uko kuli ŵanthu ŵa fuko la Tumbuku, kweni matchalitchi gha Cikhristu na vyakurya vya mazuŵa ghano vikulutilira kuyuzga ŵanthu. (Nkhani yose...)

Chithuzi cha mwahuno
Chipilala cha khomo chakuchemeka Durdle Door (panji kuti Durdle Dor) ni malo agho ghali pamaji pa Jurassic Coast kufupi na Lulworth, Dorset, England. Palije mabuku ghakwambilira agho ghakulongosora vya chiwunjiko ichi, nangauli chili na zina ilo likwenera kuti likaŵako vyaka vyakujumpha 1,000 ivyo vyajumpha. Kuumaliro wa vyaka vya m'ma 1800, pakalembeka mazgu ghakuti "mphete yikuru ya Durdle-rock Door". Mu 1811 mapu ghakwambilira gha Ordnance Survey gha malo agha ghakaŵa na zina lakuti "Dirdale Door". Durdle yikufuma ku lizgu la Chingelezi chakale lakuti thirl, kung'anamura kujima, kuchima panji kuchimba, icho chikufuma ku lizgu lakuti thyrel, kung'anamura chibowo.
Vithuzi vyakumasinda ivyo vilikuonekapo: Balambala — Tulsi — Mircha
