Principles[edit]
Every wiki with checkusers should be treated as a large wiki. However, some of those wikis may need help. Global sysops must ask communities for permission to use their rights on that project.
All projects with more than 50 000 articles and more than 10 active sysops are considered to be self-sufficient unless the project declares otherwise. However, some projects, which don't have both, may have active enough communities and global sysops should respect their decisions.
Global sysops are allowed to fight vandalism on metaprojects (see the list below)
All other wikis are treated by default as small wikis. However, a number of wikis without CUs are able to take care about themselves. Borderline cases should be discussed here (at the talk page) with all interested parties. To be qualified as a large wiki, projects without checkusers shouldn't go without any human activity more than 2 hours per day.
