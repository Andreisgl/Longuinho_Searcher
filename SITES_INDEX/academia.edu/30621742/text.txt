Texts from the Third and Second Millennium BCNicla  De ZorziThe small cuneiform collection edited here consists of five archival texts, one Ur III “messenger text” from Girsu-Lagaš, two letters and one document in Old Assyrian, and one Old Babylonian Palastgeschäft text from Larsa. Their owner, the Austrian entrepreneur Josef Zeppetzauer, acquired them in 2008 at an auction from the estate of the Austrian architect Clemens Holzmeister (1886-1983).
