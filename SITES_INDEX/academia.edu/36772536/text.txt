He suggests that it is extremely difficult, if not impossible, to have a functioning, relevant, engaged, and critical democracy without a functioning, relevant, engaged and critical education. In order to lay the groundwork, Dr. Carr will begin his presentation by elucidating some notions of democracy, some myths and debates. Dr. Carr will then present three digressions (racism, the environment and social media) that may appear to be disconnected and out-of-sync. The last section of the presentation focuses on education for democracy, and, significantly, what I/we can do or might think about doing. Some proposals based on the research Dr. Carr has been involved in related to education for democracy are also presented.
