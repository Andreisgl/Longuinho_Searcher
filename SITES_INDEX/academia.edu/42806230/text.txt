First, the author has asked the question about the theoretical basis for Scriptural Reasoning. Then he tries to explore SR from theory to practice. And he continues to study the integrity between theory and practice. And the social implications of Scriptural Reasoning has also studied before the author offers his final conclusion. Key Words: SR, theoretical basis, from theory to practice, integrity between theory and practice, social implications.
