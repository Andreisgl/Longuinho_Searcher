For non-Divehi/Mahl-speakers
The Wikimedia projects are multilingual, with wikis in dozens of languages being actively worked on and plenty more ready to go.
This Wikimedia Embassy is a central place for resources to help with cross-language issues — site-wide policy and software decisions that affect all of us and interlanguage linking.
If you'd like to help, please set up an equivalent page on your own language's wiki and link them together, and list yourself as a Wikimedia Ambassador below.
Request for ambassadors: To indicate your language skill, please consider putting Babel templates on your user page on Meta. Subscribing to the foundation-l mailinglist is also highly recommended.
