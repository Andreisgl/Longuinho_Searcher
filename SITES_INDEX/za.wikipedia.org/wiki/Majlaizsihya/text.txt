Majlaizsihya
Majlaizsihya, doenghnamzya guekgya. Miz 13 aen couh caeuq 30 aen lienzbangh sohguenjcih, gijgvangj dwg 329,845 bingzfueng goenghleix. Soujduh dwg Gizlungzpoh, cwngci cunghsim youq Buqcwngz. Majlaizsihya miz song anj, Sae Majlaizsihya caeuq Daigoz laenzgaenh, Doeng Majlaizsihya youq baihbaek Bozlozcouh. Aen mbwn gvi ndiepdaih yijlinz heiqhaeuh. Cwngdij dwg Sihminjsi cidu.
