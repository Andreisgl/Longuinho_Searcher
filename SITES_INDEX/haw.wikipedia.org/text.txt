ʻatikala i hōʻike ʻiaʻatikala i hōʻike ʻia
ʻO ka ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi (ʻŌlelo Pelekania: Hawaiian language) — ua mea ʻōlelo makuahine a ka poʻe maoli o ka pae moku ʻo Hawaiʻi, ma laila wale nō kahi e ʻōlelo ʻia nei ia ʻōlelo, no ka mea, ʻaʻole nui ka poʻe ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi i kēia mau lā. Wahi a kekahi mau anamanaʻo aupuni, he 8,000-15,000 nā mea [[1]] hiki ke ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi. He ʻōlelo kūhelu ka ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi me ka ʻōlelo Pelekania ma ka mokuʻāina ʻo Hawaiʻi wale nō. ʻAʻole nui nā mokuʻāina ʻē aʻe me ʻelua ua ʻoi paha ʻōlelo kūhelu.
ʻatikala maikaʻiʻatikala maikaʻi
ʻO ka ʻŌlelo Pelekania a i ʻole ka ʻōlelo pelekane (English language) ua mea kekahi ʻōlelo hoʻohana nui ʻia e nā kānaka a puni ke ao. Ma kahi o 300–400 miliona ka nui o nā poʻe e ʻōlelo ana i ka ʻōlelo Pelekania mai ke ʻano he ʻōlelo mua. Ma kahi o hoʻokahi piliona ka nui o nā poʻe mai, hiki ke ʻōlelo pelekania.

ʻO nā ʻano hana a pau o ka pepehi kanaka, makemake ka mea haku mele nui i ka "Russian roulette".
Kanaka hoihoiKanaka o ka lā
Ua hānau ʻia ʻo Joseph Robinette Biden ma Nowemapa 20, 1942 ma Scranton, United States. He Pelekikena Pelekikena ʻo ia no ʻAmelika Hui Pū ʻIa mai 2009 a 2017 a laila ua koho ʻia i Pelekikena o ʻAmelika Hui Pū ʻIa ma Ianuali 20, 2021.

E pili ana
He puke noiʻi kūʻikena kūnoa ʻo Wikipikia. E ʻoluʻolu nō, e hāʻawi mai i kāu ʻike, kāu manaʻo, a me kou leo no ke kūkulu ʻana a me ke kākoʻo ʻana mai i ka Wikipikia Hawaiʻi. He kahua pūnaewele Hawaiʻi kēia no ka hoʻoulu ʻana i ka ʻike Hawaiʻi. Inā hiki iā ʻoe ke ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi, e ʻoluʻolu nō, e kōkua mai a e hoʻololi i nā ʻatikala ma ʻaneʻi, a pono e haʻi aku i kou mau hoa aloha e pili ana i ka Wikipikia Hawaiʻi. E ola mau nō ka ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi a mau loa aku.
