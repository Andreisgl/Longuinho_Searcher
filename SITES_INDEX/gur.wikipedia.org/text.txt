Bolgatanga Senior High School
Bɔlegataŋa kɔmbibesi sukuu katɛ 'Bolgatanga Senior High School (BIG BOSS) bo la Upper East Region, Ghana. Bɔlegataŋa kɔmbibesi sukuu katɛ la deti ti ba wi'ira ti 'Bolgatanga Senior High School (BIG BOSS)' la, dela kɔmbibesi sukuu katɛ wia daana ti a bɔna upper east. Bɔlegataŋa kɔmbibesi sukuu katɛ la yuum pɔsɛ la '15 October, 1970'. Sukuu la yuum pɔsɛ mɛ la kɔma piyopɔi la ba nuu. Pa'aleba yuum dela pia la ayima deti Mr. Bayala C. J. Yibrul dɛna sukuu la zuo daana 'first Heamaster'.
