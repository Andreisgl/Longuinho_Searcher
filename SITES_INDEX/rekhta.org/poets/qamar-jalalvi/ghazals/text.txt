kabhi kaha na kisi se tere fasane ko
kab mera nasheman ahl-e-chaman gulshan mein gawara karte hain
na rukte hain aansu na thamte hain nale
unki taraf se tark-e-mulaqat ho gai
mera KHamosh rah kar bhi unhen sab kuchh suna dena
ai mere ham-nashin chal kahin aur chal is chaman mein ab apna guzara nahin
sajde tere kahne se main kar lun bhi to kya ho
kisi ka nam lo be-nam afsane bahut se hain
husn kab ishq ka mamnun-e-wafa hota hai
tumko hum KHak-nashinon ka KHayal aane tak
musa samjhe the arman nikal jaega
barish mein ahd toD ke gar mai-kashi hui
raaz-e-dil kyun na kahun samne diwanon ke
shaiKH aaKHir ye surahi hai koi KHum to nahin
ye raste mein kis se mulaqat kar li
haTi zulf un ke chehre se magar aahista aahista
unhen kyun phul dushman eid mein pahnae jate hain
surKHiyan kyun DhunDh kar laun fasane ke liye
wo aaghaz-e-mohabbat ka zamana
tauba kije ab fareb-e-dosti khaenge kya
abru to dikha dijiye shamshir se pahle
karenge shikwa-e-jaur-o-jafa dil khol kar apna
ab kaise rafu pairahan ho is aawara diwane ka
chhoD kar ghar-bar apna hasrat-e-didar mein
soz-e-gham-e-firaq se dil ko bachae kaun
ye roz hashr ka aur shikwa-e-wafa ke liye
aah ko samjhe ho kya dil se agar ho jaegi
kabhi jo aankh pe gesu-e-yar hota hai
isMein koi fareb to ai aasman nahin
kisi surat sahar nahin hoti
lahad aur hashr mein ye farq kam pae nahin jate
tum apni yaad se kah do na jae chhoD ke dil
ba-juz tumhaare kisi se koi sawal nahin
mujhe baghban se gila ye hai ki chaman se be-KHabari rahi
dekhiye ho gai badnam masihai bhi
dekhte hain raqs mein din raat paimane ko hum
kaun se the wo tumhaare ahd jo TuTe na the
karte bhi kya huzur na jab apne ghar mile
sawal chhoD ki haalat ye kyun banai hai
agar chhuTa bhi us se aaina-KHana to kya hoga
duhai hai teri tu le KHabar o la-makan wale
tere nisar na dekhi koi KHushi maine
ab to munh se bol mujhko dekh din bhar ho gaya
ye dard-e-hijr aur is par sahar nahin hoti
pite hi surKH aankhen hain mast-e-sharab ki
yun tumhaare na-tawan-e-shauq manzil bhar chale
bagh-e-alam mein rahe shadi-o-matam ki tarah
dil agar hota to mil jata nishan-e-arzu
sans un ke mariz-e-hasrat ki ruk ruk ke chalti jati hai
donon hain un ke hijr ka hasil liye hue
