Asturian[edit]
Noun[edit]
restu m (plural restos)
rest (remaining part)
Esperanto[edit]
Verb[edit]
restu
imperative of resti
Malay[edit]
Etymology[edit]
Rebracketing of Sanskrit सिद्धिर् अस्तु (siddhir astu, “let it be perfect”).
Pronunciation[edit]
Rhymes: -u
Noun[edit]
restu (Jawi spelling رستو‎, plural restu-restu, informal 1st possessive restuku, 2nd possessive restumu, 3rd possessive restunya)
a blessing from God
permission
Synonyms[edit]
rahmat, rezeki, berkat (blessing)
izin (permission)
