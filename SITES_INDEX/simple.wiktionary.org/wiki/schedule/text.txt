Pronunciation[change]

Audio (US)(file)
Noun[change]
Singular
schedule
Plural
schedules
A school schedule
(countable & uncountable) A schedule is a plan of things that are happening and the times when they happen. Synonym: plan
He visits each village in his or her area on a weekly schedule.
The train is right on schedule.
The meeting was running 10 minutes ahead of schedule.
His doctors told him to cut down on his work schedule.
(countable) (formal) A schedule is a list, usually one with prices.
Please see the enclosed schedule of costs for facilities and activities.
Verb[change]

schedule

Past tense

Past participle
scheduled
Present participle
scheduling
(transitive) If you schedule something, you make a plan for when and where it will happen. Synonym: plan
The party was originally scheduled for June 5th.
