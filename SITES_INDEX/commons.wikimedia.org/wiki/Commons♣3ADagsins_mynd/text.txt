Dagsins mynd
1 september 2023
Panoramic view (span of approx. 150°) of the city of Cusco, Peru. The city, located near the Sacred Valley of the Andes mountain range, is the capital of the Cusco Province, has a population of 428,450 inhabitants and its elevation is around 3,400 metres (11,200 ft). The site was the historic capital of the Inca Empire from the 13th until the 16th-century Spanish conquest and was declared in 1983 a UNESCO World Heritage Site.+/− [fo], +/− [en]
Niðanfyri sæst eitt yvirlit yvir tær myndir, ið verða vístar sum Dagsins mynd á forsíðuni frá Commons. Henda síðan gevur møguleika fyri týðing av myndatekstinum til so mong mál sum gjørligt.
Allar dagsins myndir mugu vera Útvaldar myndir á Commons. (Útvaldar myndir verða valdar eftir góðskukriterium, ið semja fæst um her). Vel, um gjørligt, myndir frá teimum seinast valdu, ið ikki longu hava verið dagsins mynd.
