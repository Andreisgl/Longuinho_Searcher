Mei he Wikipedia
ʻOku talitali lelei koe ki he Wikipedia,
Ko e tohi ʻilo tauʻatāina ʻa ia ʻe lava ke ʻetitaʻi ʻe ha taha pe.
Kuo mau faʻu ha ngaahi fakamatala ʻe 1,981 talu mei hono fakaava ʻo e Wikipedia Tonga..
Kātaki lesisita kapau ʻoku teʻeki ke ke fai ia!.
Sai ʻaupito, ko koe ʻoku kau maí. ʻE lava ke ke kamata fakatonutonu.
Ko e kotoa ʻo ha'a tangata ʻoku fānauʻi mai ʻoku tauʻatāina pea tatau ʻi he ngeia mo e ngaahi totonu. Naʻe fakanāunauʻi kinautolu ʻaki ʻa e ʻatamai mo e konisenisi pea ʻoku totonu ke nau feohi ʻi he laumālie ʻo e nofo fakatautehina.
Ko e Uikipetia ʻeni maʻa lea faka-Tonga, ʻi Polinisia, mo e muli.
